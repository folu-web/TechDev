About Us


At LetsGo Travel, with locations in Calgary, Edmonton and Surrey, we have a team of travel agents ready to help you plan your perfect trip! We’ve been serving our Western Canada clients for over 30 years with everything from finding the best airfare rates to acquiring the right travel insurance and documentation. We strive to be available to our clients at any time to help out if any unplanned circumstances arise on your trip. So, whether you’re looking to acquire a work visa or you are planning your dream trip through the Mediterranean, we can help! Give us a call today, or send us a message on our convenient eform.
